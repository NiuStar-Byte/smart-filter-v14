## Architecture Decisions (Locked)

### ❌ What Failed
- Cron jobs + watchdogs (caused independent process failures)
- Complex daemon → spawner → child model (crashes, stale processes)
- Separate process management (no unified health checks)

### ✅ What Works
- **Unified Supervisor** (pec_master_controller.py)
  - Single entry point managing all daemons
  - Continuous 10-second health checks
  - Auto-restart with max 5 retries
  - One critical alert process for all
  
- **Persistent Loop Architecture**
  - Simple continuous loops (no spawning)
  - Self-healing error handling
  - Single PID per daemon (easy monitoring)
  - Lower memory/CPU footprint

- **Two-Daemon Model**
  - main.py: Generates new signals
  - pec_executor_persistent.py: Processes signals (closes, calculates P&L, assigns tiers)
  - Both run forever until manually killed
  - No worker threads, no child processes
