## 🎯 WHAT IS PEC?

**PEC = Post-Entry Control**

It's a **backtesting system that simulates trading** on signals generated by SmartFilter:
1. Takes a signal fired by SmartFilter (symbol, timeframe, entry price, direction)
2. Looks at what happened **AFTER** the signal fired
3. Calculates exit price based on TP/SL targets
4. Determines if trade was WIN, LOSS, or BREAK EVEN
5. Aggregates results to measure **signal quality & profitability**

---

## 🔄 HOW THE SYSTEM WORKS (Step-by-Step)

### **STEP 1: SmartFilter Fires a Signal**

```
Example:
- Time: 2026-02-22 15:30:00 UTC
- Symbol: BTC-USDT
- Timeframe: 15min
- Direction: LONG
- Entry Price: $42,500.00
- Confidence Score: 18/20 (90%)
```

**Signal is logged to:** `fired_signals_temp.csv` (or internal tracking)

### **STEP 2: PEC Captures Signal Data**

PEC extracts:
- ✅ symbol
- ✅ fired_time_utc (when signal was generated)
- ✅ entry_price (best bid/ask at signal time)
- ✅ signal_type (LONG or SHORT)
- ✅ timeframe (15min, 30min, 1h)
- ✅ entry_idx (position in OHLCV bar array)

### **STEP 3: PEC Fetches OHLCV Data**

Retrieves 250 candles for that symbol/timeframe:
- Last 20 bars BEFORE signal = market context
- ~20 bars AFTER signal = exit zone (for TP/SL detection)

```
Example:
- Get BTC-USDT 15min candles (250 bars)
- Timestamps: 2026-02-20 to 2026-02-22
- PEC identifies the bar matching signal time
```

### **STEP 4: PEC Simulates Exit (TP/SL Logic)**

Starting from entry, PEC scans forward for:

**For LONG signals:**
```
- Target Profit (TP): Entry + 1.5% = $43,140
- Stop Loss (SL): Entry - 1.0% = $42,075
- Max Hold: 20 bars (300 min = 5 hours)

Scan bars AFTER signal:
  Bar 1: HIGH $42,600 - No TP hit yet
  Bar 2: HIGH $43,200 - ✅ HIT TP! Exit at $43,140
  
Result: WIN (+1.5%, -0.2% fees = +1.3% net)
```

**For SHORT signals:**
```
- Target Profit (TP): Entry - 1.5%
- Stop Loss (SL): Entry + 1.0%
- Same 20-bar max hold

Scan bars AFTER signal:
  Bar 1: LOW $42,400 - No SL hit
  Bar 2: LOW $42,050 - ✅ HIT SL! Exit at $42,075
  
Result: LOSS (-1.0%, -0.2% fees = -1.2% total)
```

**Exit Reasons:**
- `TP`: Took profit (WIN) 🟢
- `SL`: Hit stop loss (LOSS) 🔴
- `TIMEOUT`: Held 20 bars without TP/SL (BREAK EVEN) 🟡

### **STEP 5: Calculate P&L**

For each signal:
```
PnL = (Exit Price - Entry Price) × Shares - Fees
Win Rate = Wins / Total Signals
Avg Win = Average profit per winning trade
Avg Loss = Average loss per losing trade
```

### **STEP 6: Generate Report**

Output includes:
```
- Total Signals Backtested: N
- Wins: X (X%)
- Losses: Y (Y%)
- Break-Even: Z (Z%)
- Total P&L: $ABC
- Filter Effectiveness: Score
```

## 🎯 HOW TO INTERPRET RESULTS

### **Win Rate**

```
Win Rate = (Wins) / (Wins + Losses) × 100%

Examples:
- 60/100 signals = 60% win rate ✅ EXCELLENT
- 55/100 signals = 55% win rate ✅ ACCEPTABLE
- 45/100 signals = 45% win rate ❌ NEEDS TUNING
```

**Target:** ≥ 55% (profitable with proper risk management)

### **P&L**

```
P&L = (Profits from Wins) - (Losses from Losses) - (All Fees)

Positive P&L = More money won than lost
Negative P&L = Losing system (needs fixing)
```

**Target:** Positive P&L (even small gains are good at start)

### **False Positives**

```
False Positive = Signal that results in a LOSS

Examples:
- 50 signals fired
- 29 wins, 21 losses
- False positive rate = 21/50 = 42%

After Profitability v1 deployment, target: <30% false positives
```

- Max hold: 20 bars (300 min = 5 hours on 15min)

- Realistic? ✅ Real trades don't hold forever
