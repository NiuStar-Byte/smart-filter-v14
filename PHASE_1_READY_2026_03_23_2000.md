## Strategy Summary

**Three-Layer Safety Net (Instead of Single Halt):**

1. **Real-Time Detection** (Seconds)
   - Verify immediately after write
   - Failure logged and alerted instantly
   - Signals keep firing ✓

2. **Background Monitoring** (Phase 2 - 5 minutes)
   - Thread checks every 5 min
   - Detects divergence within 5 min
   - Alerts ops if gap grows

3. **Automatic Recovery** (Phase 3 - 5-10 minutes)
   - If gap > 50 signals, auto-backfill
   - Hourly checkpoint cron
   - Fixes without manual action

**Result:** 
- ✅ Trading never stops
- ✅ Issues detected immediately
- ✅ Auto-fixed in background
- ✅ Operators notified
- ✅ Production ready
