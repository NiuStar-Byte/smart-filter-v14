## Key Design: Alert + Continue (NOT Halt)

**Why this matters:**
- ✅ Signals NEVER stop (even if writes fail)
- ✅ Failures detected in seconds
- ✅ Operators alerted immediately
- ✅ Auto-recovery in background (Phases 2-3)
- ✅ Production ready

**Unlike "Halt" approach:**
- ❌ Would stop daemon if write fails
- ❌ Requires manual restart
- ❌ Misses market opportunities
- ❌ Not suitable for trading
